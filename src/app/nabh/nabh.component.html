NABH Accredited
Aksha Hospital has achieved NABH accreditation, awarded by the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers (NABH), a part of the Quality Council of India. This accreditation reflects our commitment to delivering high-quality healthcare services and prioritizing patient safety through rigorous standards in care, infection control, and operational practices.
Benefits of NABH Accreditation for Our Patients:
  • Enhanced Patient Safety: Implementation of standardized protocols minimizes risks and ensures a safe environment for patients.
  • Quality Care: Adherence to evidence-based practices leads to improved clinical outcomes and patient satisfaction.
  • Transparency: Clear communication and respect for patient rights foster trust and confidence in our services.
  • Continuous Improvement: Regular assessments and feedback mechanisms drive ongoing enhancements in care delivery.
We believe that NABH accreditation is not just a certification but a continuous journey toward excellence. We are dedicated to upholding these standards and consistently improving our services to meet and exceed patient expectations. Your health and well-being are our top priorities, and we are committed to providing you with the highest quality of care.
