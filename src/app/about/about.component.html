ABOUT AKSHA HOSPITAL
Aksha Hospital, a 50-bedded multi-specialty hospital located in Jayanagar, Bengaluru. Founded by Dr. Bhoopal with the vision of providing compassionate, ethical, and affordable healthcare services. Our hospital is dedicated in offering world-class medical care across various specialties, including gastroenterology, obstetrics, gynecology, pediatrics, orthopaedics and more. Our highly experienced team of doctors and specialists ensures that each patient receives the highest standard of care and attention.
At Aksha Hospital, we are committed to delivering holistic, accessible and integrated healthcare. Our approach prioritizes the well-being of our patients by using the state-of-art facilities and cutting-edge medical technology. We strive to make global healthcare services innovative, affordable, and within reach of every patient. Our focus on compassionate care is at the heart of everything we do, ensuring that every professional at our hospital is dedicated to delivering quality service and fostering a welcoming environment for routine checkups as well as specialized treatments.
The hospital is commmited in providing innovative healthcare services, comprehensive and safe facilities and a responsive environment. Our core values include treating their patients with the utmost compassion, integrity, and respect while also focusing on learning and innovation. We know that our patients have a busy schedule, and we value your time. Therefore, we pledge to ensure that our patients get treated by a healthcare professional as soon as possible. We look at things as our goal, and we continuously strive to exceed them. We want to empower our patients so that they can make decisions regarding their health care. To achieve that, we make sure that we feed information to our patients with the current medical information on the diagnosis and treatment. We also encourage our patients to ask questions regarding their treatment regimen and other related doubts. Every medical professional in the hospital is dedicated to finding complete solutions to our patient’s medical condition. Rather than mitigate the symptoms, we tend to address the issue and seek solutions beyond the traditional method. The hospital also provides its patients with sophisticated medical evaluation facilities; we make use of state-of-the-art technology for performing several screening procedures at our hospital. We present these tests and procedures at the time of the patient’s appointment to avoid the need for multiple dates for the patient.
