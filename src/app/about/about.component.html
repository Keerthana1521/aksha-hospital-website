ABOUT AKSHA HOSPITAL
Aksha Hospital is located in Jayanagar, Bengaluru. The hospital was set up with a vision of providing compassionate, ethical, innovative and affordable global healthcare services. We hope to achieve this by making the best use of their resources and bringing about innovation in the field of medicine.
Our team wants to bring in holistic, accessible and integrated healthcare to the doorstep of every patient, and strongly believe in the idea of providing care with compassion.
Every professional at the hospital is committed to providing world-class healthcare by encouraging their highly qualified professionals to deliver quality service.
The hospital hopes to provide innovative healthcare services, comprehensive and safe facilities and a responsive environment. Our core values include treating their patients with the utmost compassion, integrity, and respect while also focusing on learning and innovation. We know that our patients have a busy schedule, and we value your time. Therefore, we pledge to ensure that our patients get seen by a healthcare professional as soon as possible. We look at things as our goal, and we continuously strive to exceed them. We want to empower our patients so that they can make decisions regarding their health care. To achieve that, we make sure that we feed information to our patients with the current medical information on the diagnosis and treatment. We also encourage our patients to ask questions regarding their treatment regimen and other related doubts. Every medical professional in the hospital is dedicated to finding complete solutions to our patient’s medical condition. Rather than mitigate the symptoms, we tend to address the issue and seek solutions beyond the traditional method. The hospital also provides its patients with sophisticated medical evaluation facilities; we make use of state-of-the-art technology for performing several screening procedures at our hospital. We present these tests and procedures at the time of the patient’s appointment to avoid the need for multiple dates for the patient.
